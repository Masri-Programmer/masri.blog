## 🛠️ Marketplace Listing Ecosystem
Ranalp features four distinct listing types, each optimized for specific user needs and legal requirements.

### 1. Private Occasions 🔐
*   **Best for:** Birthdays, weddings, and family events.
*   **Privacy:** Accessible only via direct invitation links (WhatsApp, SMS, Email).
*   **Flexibility:** All funds are retained even if the target goal is not fully met.

### 2. Founders & Creative People 💡
*   **Best for:** Startups and creative minds in the early R&D phase.
*   **Model:** Donation-based support without equity exchange.
*   **Transparency:** Requires detailed fund usage breakdowns and regular progress updates.

### 3. Non-Profit / Donation Campaigns 🏥
*   **Best for:** Registered NGOs and verified associations.
*   **Security:** Only verified entities can launch these campaigns.
*   **Impact:** Direct, periodical payouts to official association bank accounts.

### 4. Charitable Auctions 🔨
*   **Best for:** Unique products and experiences sold for a good cause.
*   **Methods:** Supports both **Live Bidding** (Auctions) and **Fixed Price** (Buy Now).
*   **Integration:** Proceeds are automatically routed to linked donation campaigns.

## 💻 Technical Infrastructure: Fintech Power
The heart of Ranalp is a robust financial engine built to handle complex multi-party transactions.

> [!tip] Stripe Connect Integration
> We utilized **Stripe Connect** to manage the entire payout lifecycle. This enables:
> - **KYC Verification:** Seamless onboarding for individuals and associations.
> - **Automated Split-Payments:** Directing proceeds to charity while managing platform fees.
> - **Global Compliance:** Adhering to strict financial regulations for crowdfunding in the DACH region.
