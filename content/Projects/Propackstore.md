[Propackstore.org](https://propackstore.org/home) is an online store specializing in **personalized sports cards, particularly "FUT cards"** (likely referring to cards inspired by popular football/soccer video games like FIFA/EA Sports FC Ultimate Team).

**Core Business and Products:**

- **Personalized Sports Cards:** The main offering of Propackstore.org is custom-made cards that allow users to "shine like stars" by featuring their own photo, name, and customized statistics or details on a card design.
- **Variety of Sports:** While "FUT cards" are a primary focus, they offer designs for over 100 cards across various sports, including Football, Basketball, Tennis, and more. This suggests a broad appeal to sports fans who want a unique, personalized item.
- **Customization:** Customers can personalize various aspects of their chosen card design, creating a unique product.
- **Worldwide Delivery:** The website explicitly states that custom cards can be delivered to customers worldwide.

**Mission and Values:**

- **Mission:** Propack's objective is to "reach every home around the world." They aim to "take part of your journey and bring every idea to life," focusing on covering a wide range of sports globally.
- **Values:** They emphasize delivering the product customers paid for within the committed timeframe. Their goal is to "put smiles on faces" when packages are opened and to exceed expectations by providing high-quality cards.

**How it Works (Customer Journey):**

1. **Pick a Card:** Customers choose from over 100 specialized designs.
2. **Customize:** They then personalize the chosen card.
3. **Place Order:** A preview of the customized card is provided for approval before the order is finalized and shipped.
