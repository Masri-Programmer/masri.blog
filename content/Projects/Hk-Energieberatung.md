The website for HK Energieberatung is about energy consulting services for residential buildings. They are a team of certified energy consultants specializing in efficient, economical, and sustainable solutions.

Key services they offer include:

- Creating energy performance certificates
    
- Conducting hydraulic balancing
    
- Assisting with applications for subsidies from organizations like BAFA and KfW.
    

The website also highlights their expertise in navigating various subsidy programs, such as the Individual Renovation Roadmap (iSFP), which can secure a 5% extra bonus. They also provide a transparent cost structure, offering a free initial consultation, a popular energy consulting package that includes the iSFP for a reduced price of €650, and energy certificates starting from €70. The company is based in Bremen, Germany.
