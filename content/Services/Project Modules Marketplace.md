# Marketplace Platform Modules

A comprehensive list of modules designed for multi-vendor marketplaces and e-commerce platforms, categorized by industry focus.

---

## 🏗️ Core Marketplace Modules
*These modules form the foundation of a multi-vendor marketplace platform.*

- **Vendor Management:** Seller/supplier accounts, moderation, limits, and payout management. `Recommended`
- **Product Catalog & Inventory:** Multi-vendor catalogs, attributes, categories, and stock tracking. `Recommended`
- **Orders & Fulfillment:** End-to-end order management, delivery tracking, returns, and refunds. `Recommended`
- **Payments:** Secure payment gateways, split payments, and transaction handling. `Recommended`
- **Reviews & Ratings:** User evaluations, moderation, and aggregate scoring for products/vendors. `Recommended`
- **Company Page:** Vendor profiles with descriptions, ratings, contacts, and verification status.
- **Admin Dashboard (CMS):** Centralized back-office for content, users, roles, and system settings. `Recommended`
- **3rd Party Integration:** Logistics, CRM, and financial API integrations. `Recommended`
- **Analytics & Reporting:** Sales tracking, vendor performance, and customer insights.
- **Search & Filters:** Faceted search, sorting, and advanced discovery.

## ⚙️ General Platform Modules
*Standard modules included in most enterprise applications.*

- **User Management & Auth:** SSO, 2FA, and secure account management.
- **User Profile & Settings:** Personalization, localization, and preferences.
- **Notifications System:** Multi-channel alerts (Email, SMS, Push).
- **Organizations & Multi-Tenancy:** Workspace isolation and organizational data management.
- **ChatBot Builder:** Basic AI/flow builder for automated customer support.
