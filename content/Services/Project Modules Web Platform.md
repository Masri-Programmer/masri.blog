# Web Platform Modules

A comprehensive list of modules for general-purpose web platforms and SaaS applications, categorized by industry focus.

---

## 🏗️ Core Platform Modules
*These modules define the user experience and integration capabilities of a modern web platform.*

- **User Onboarding & In-App Help:** Interactive tutorials, tooltips, walkthroughs, and contextual FAQs. `Recommended`
- **Search & Filters:** Global search with advanced facets, sorting, and filtered discovery. `Recommended`
- **Knowledge Base:** Centralized documentation, categorized FAQs, and semantic search. `Recommended`
- **3rd Party Integration:** Robust API management, webhooks, data sync, and OAuth. `Recommended`
- **Analytics & Reporting:** KPI dashboards, event tracking, and data exports. `Recommended`
- **Admin Dashboard (Back Office):** Full CRUD control, system settings, and audit logs. `Recommended`
- **Organizations & Multi-Tenancy:** Secure data isolation at the organizational or workspace level.

## 🏢 Industry Specific Modules

### **FinTech**
- **Payments:** Payment gateways, transaction processing, and status tracking. `Recommended`
- **Wallet:** Multi-currency support, user balances, and top-up/withdrawal logic. `Recommended`
- **Transactions History:** Financial logs, audit trails, and exportable reports. `Recommended`
- **KYC/KYB & Compliance:** Identity verification and AML/regulatory checks.
- **Risk & Fraud Detection:** Scoring rules, anomaly detection, and automated blocking.

### **Education**
- **Content Feed/Timeline:** Activity streams with social interactions (likes/comments). `Recommended`
- **Quiz / Voting:** Interactive assessments, polls, and real-time results. `Recommended`
- **Video Conferencing:** Live streams, webinars, and recording management. `Recommended`
- **Booking & Scheduling:** Tutor/mentor availability, calendars, and time slots.

### **Logistics**
- **Fleet & Tracking:** Shipment routes, driver tracking, and last-mile management. `Recommended`
- **Maps & Geolocation:** Geocoding, routing, and address validation. `Recommended`
- **Route Optimization:** Cost/time optimization and constraint-based routing. `Recommended`
- **Delivery ETA:** Real-time updates and delay predictions. `Recommended`
- **Driver Management:** Performance tracking, schedules, and assignments.

### **HR / Recruitment**
- **Applicant Tracking System (ATS):** Candidate pipeline and hiring stage management. `Recommended`
- **Resume Parser:** AI-driven skill extraction and candidate ranking. `Recommended`
- **Employee Onboarding:** New hire workflows, document management, and provisioning.
- **Booking & Scheduling:** Interview scheduling and calendar synchronization.

### **Marketing**
- **Subscriptions & Billing:** Plan management, recurring invoices, and billing cycles. `Recommended`
- **Campaign Management:** Multi-channel creation, targeting, and tracking. `Recommended`
- **Lead Scoring:** Evaluation rules, segmentation, and CRM synchronization.

### **Real Estate**
- **Property Listing:** Media-rich property data, filters, and availability. `Recommended`
- **Property Comparison:** Side-by-side analysis of key parameters. `Recommended`
- **Property Search:** Advanced real estate discovery and search results. `Recommended`
- **Virtual Tours:** 3D/VR tours and interactive media hosting.
- **Mortgage Calculator:** Loan estimates and affordability calculations.

### **Healthcare**
- **Patient Portal & EHR:** Secure record access and EHR synchronization. `Recommended`
- **Telemedicine:** Secure video consultations and integrated patient chat. `Recommended`
- **Prescription Management:** E-prescriptions and medication tracking.
- **Lab Results Integration:** Data import, notifications, and results display.
- **Booking & Scheduling:** Appointment management and reminders. `Recommended`

### **Travel & Hospitality**
- **Maps & Geolocation:** POIs, route planning, and address management. `Recommended`
- **Booking & Scheduling:** Reservation management and availability calendars. `Recommended`
- **Content Feed:** Social activity and destination updates. `Recommended`

## ⚙️ General Platform Modules
*Standard modules included in most enterprise applications.*

- **User Management & Auth:** SSO, 2FA, and secure account recovery.
- **User Profile & Settings:** Personalization, localization, and notification preferences.
- **Notifications System:** Multi-channel alerts (Email, SMS, Push).
- **Product Catalog & Inventory:** Attributes, categories, and stock management.
- **Reviews & Ratings:** Feedback moderation and aggregate scoring.
- **Orders & Fulfillment:** End-to-end order lifecycle management.
- **ChatBot Builder:** Basic AI-driven support or flow-based responses.
