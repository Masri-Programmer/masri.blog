> [!question] What can the calculator do?
> This tool helps you accurately project your website expenses so you can confidently build them into your marketing budget. It covers standard requirements like design, development, and common integrations.

> [!question] Is the calculation 100% accurate?
> The result provides a highly reliable estimate based on common industry parameters. However, keep in mind that highly customized requests, unique animations, or complex third-party integrations can influence the final cost.

> [!tip] Planning for the long term
> Your website will likely be online for years, so it pays to find a web service provider you have great chemistry with! 
> 
> If you stick with an agency that knows your site inside and out, smaller updates can be handled quickly and affordably. While you can always change agencies, a new team will require time to familiarize themselves with custom work—finding a partner for the long haul saves both time and money.
