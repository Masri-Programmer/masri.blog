# AI Solution & Chatbot Modules

A comprehensive list of modules designed for AI-driven solutions and intelligent chatbot systems, categorized by industry focus.

---

## 🛠️ Core AI & Communication Modules
*These modules form the foundation of most AI and Chatbot solutions.*

- **AI-Powered / AI System Integration:** Built-in AI features or connection to LLM/ML services (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.).
- **ChatBot Builder:** Visual flow builder for intents, responses, and conversational logic.
- **Messaging & Chat:** Direct/group chats, inbox management, threads, and file sharing.
- **Knowledge Base:** Centralized FAQ, documentation, categories, and semantic search.
- **Private LLM Model:** Custom LLM hosting, fine-tuning, and secure model management.
- **3rd Party Integration:** External APIs, webhooks, data sync, and OAuth management.
- **Analytics & Reporting:** Event tracking, KPI dashboards, and data exports.
- **Organizations & Multi-Tenancy:** Workspace isolation and organizational data management.

## ⚙️ General Platform Modules
*Standard modules included in most enterprise applications.*

- **User Management & Auth:** SSO, 2FA, and secure account management.
- **User Profile & Settings:** Personalization, localization, and preferences.
- **Notifications System:** Multi-channel alerts (Email, SMS, Push).
- **Admin Dashboard (CMS):** Back-office control for content and system settings.
- **Product Catalog & Inventory:** Attributes, categories, and stock management.
- **Search & Filters:** Global search with advanced filtering.
- **Reviews & Ratings:** User feedback moderation and aggregation.
- **Orders & Fulfillment:** End-to-end order lifecycle management.
