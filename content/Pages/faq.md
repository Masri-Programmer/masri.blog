# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is your typical project flow?
We follow a structured 5-step process: Discovery, Design, Development, Testing, and Deployment. Each phase includes client feedback loops to ensure alignment.

## How much does a website cost?
Costs vary based on complexity. A starter landing page starts at €200, while a custom business website typically ranges from €600 to €2000.

## Can I maintain the website myself?
Yes! We provide a user-friendly CMS (Content Management System) and training so you can update text, images, and blog posts without technical knowledge.

## Which technologies do you use?
We specialize in the VILT stack (Vue.js, Inertia.js, Laravel, Tailwind CSS) for robust, high-performance web applications.

## Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Absolutely. We have monthly maintenance packages that cover security updates, backups, and small content changes to keep your site running smoothly.
