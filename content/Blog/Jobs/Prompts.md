Prompts for job finding 

- Act as an expert Executive Recruiter. Please analyze my attached resume and use the Indeed connector to find the best relative job openings currently available in Germany or Remotely. Please present the top matching results in a highly structured, professional table with the following columns: 1. Job Title 2. Company Name 3. Location (Specific city/state or Remote) 4. Job Type (Full-time / Part-time/ Contract /Remote) 5. Match Score (out of 10): How well my resume aligns with the job requirements. 6. Match Justification: A brief 1-sentence explanation of why you gave this score. 7. Skill Gap: 1-2 key skills required for the job that are missing or weak in my resume. 8. Salary Range: (If mentioned in the job post). 9. Apply Link: Direct link to the job posting.  



- Act as an expert Resume Writer and ATS Optimizer. Please select the top 3 jobs from the previous table with the highest Match Score. For each of these 3 jobs, rewrite my resume to perfectly align with its specific requirements. Ensure the new resumes are ATS-optimized by naturally incorporating the exact keywords from the job descriptions. Highlight quantifiable achievements. Crucially, address the Skill Gaps identified in the table by emphasizing relevant transferable skills from my existing experience. 
  
- Next, write a highly tailored and compelling cover letter for each of those top 3 jobs. The cover letters must NOT sound like generic, robotic AI templates. Use a strong opening, directly address the company's core needs as mentioned in the job posting, and articulate how my specific experience in AI Product Management can solve their problems. Keep each letter concise, persuasive, and strictly under 300 words.
