Head and Shoulders: A bearish reversal pattern. After an uptrend, this pattern means down.

  

Inverse Head and Shoulders: A bullish reversal pattern. After a downtrend, this pattern means up.
  

Double Top: A bearish reversal pattern. After an uptrend, this pattern means down.



Double Bottom: A bullish reversal pattern. After a downtrend, this pattern means up.





Rising Wedge: A bearish pattern. Can be a reversal or continuation. Signals down.
Falling Wedge: A bullish pattern. Can be a reversal or continuation. Signals up.

  

Ascending Triangle: A bullish continuation pattern. During an uptrend, this pattern means up.
Descending Triangle: A bearish continuation pattern. During a downtrend, this pattern means down.
Symmetrical Triangle: Can be bullish or bearish, a breakout confirms the direction.

  


  

Cup and Handle: A bullish continuation pattern. During an uptrend, this pattern means up.

  

Channel Up: A bullish continuation pattern. Within an uptrend, this pattern means up.
Channel Down: A bearish continuation pattern. Within a downtrend, this pattern means down.
Sideways Channel (Range): A neutral pattern. Price moves between support and resistance. Breakout confirms direction.
