Doji: It represents indecision in the market. After an uptrend, a Doji means down. After a downtrend, a Doji means up.

Hammer: A bullish reversal pattern. After a downtrend, a Hammer means up.
Shooting Star: A bearish reversal pattern. After an uptrend, a Shooting Star means down.

Bullish Engulfing: A bullish reversal pattern. After a downtrend, this pattern means up.
Bearish Engulfing: A bearish reversal pattern. After an uptrend, this pattern means down.
