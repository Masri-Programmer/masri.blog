### Core & Essential Features

- Homepage with a Clear Value Proposition
    
- Detailed Services List
    
- About Us (Experience, Qualifications)
    
- Service Area (Map or List of Cities/Districts)
    
- Contact Page with Prominent Phone Number
    
- Mobile-First Responsive Design
    

### Lead Generation & Trust Building

- Request a Quote / Free Estimate Form
    
- Project Gallery / Portfolio (Before & After Photos)
    
- Customer Testimonials & Reviews
    
- Licenses, Certifications & Insurance Page
    
- Clear Calls-to-Action (e.g., "Call Now")
    
- Online Appointment / Service Booking
    

### Informational & Marketing

- FAQ Section
    
- Blog / Helpful Tips Section
    
- Pricing Guide or Rates Information
    
- Social Media Links
    

### Advanced & Optional Features

- Emergency Service Information / Banner
    
- Client Portal for Invoices and History
    
- Video Testimonials or Project Walkthroughs
    
- Financing Options Information
    
- Team Page (if it's a company)
