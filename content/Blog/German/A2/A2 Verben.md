## Reflexive Verben 

**Common Akkusativ Reflexive Verbs at A2:**

- _sich freuen_ (to be happy)
- _sich waschen_ (to wash oneself)
- _sich anziehen_ (to get dressed)
- _sich ausruhen_ (to rest)
- _sich beeilen_ (to hurry)
- _sich entschuldigen_ (to apologize)
- _sich fühlen_ (to feel)
- _sich informieren_ (to inform oneself)
- _sich interessieren_ (to be interested)
- _sich setzen_ (to sit down)
- _sich treffen_ (to meet)
- _sich verlieben_ (to fall in love)

**Common Dativ Reflexive Verbs at A2 (often with a body part or item):**

- _sich etwas anziehen_ (to put something on oneself)
- _sich etwas ausziehen_ (to take something off oneself)
- _sich die Zähne putzen_ (to brush one's teeth)
- _sich die Haare kämmen/bürsten_ (to comb/brush one's hair)
- _sich die Hände waschen_ (to wash one's hands)
- _sich wehtun_ (to hurt oneself - e.g., _Ich habe **mir** wehgetan._)
- _sich etwas kaufen_ (to buy oneself something)
- _sich etwas vorstellen_ (to imagine something for oneself)
