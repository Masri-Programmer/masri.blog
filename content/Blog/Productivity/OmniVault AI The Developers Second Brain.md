## OmniVault AI: The Developer's "Second Brain" and AI Command Center

In the fast-paced world of software engineering, information isn't just power—it's noise. To cut through that noise, I’ve developed **OmniVault AI**, a professional-grade Obsidian environment designed to bridge the gap between high-level knowledge management and cutting-edge AI orchestration.

Whether you're managing complex VILT stack projects or building your personal knowledge base, OmniVault AI provides the structural integrity and AI-native workflows to scale your productivity.

---

### 🛠️ The Architecture: PARA on Steroids

At its core, OmniVault AI is built on a refined version of the **PARA Method**, optimized for technical depth and clarity:

*   **10_Actions:** Where the work happens. Active projects, goal setting, and task Kanbans.
*   **20_Areas:** Long-term responsibilities like Business Operations, Learning, and Personal Finances.
*   **30_Knowledge:** A digital slipbox for literature notes, permanent insights, and Maps of Content (MOCs).
*   **40_Journal:** A high-performance logging system. It doesn’t just track dates; it tracks **Ultradian Cycles**, habits, and productivity metrics using interactive widgets.
*   **95_AI:** The "Brain" of the vault. A dedicated directory for managing AI agents, system prompts, and custom skills.

---

### 🤖 AI-Native Workflows

OmniVault AI isn't just a place to store text; it’s an **AI Command Center**. It includes specialized configurations to make your vault "AI-legible," allowing agents like Claude, Gemini, and ChatGPT to operate with surgical precision:

> [!info] **Context-Aware Agents**
> The vault includes pre-baked **System Prompts** (`CLAUDE.md`, `GPT.md`, `GEMINI.md`) that teach AI models exactly how your vault is structured. When I invoke an agent, it already knows how to format properties, where to file notes, and how to link related concepts.

#### Key AI Features:
- **Active Skills:** Custom instructions that allow AI to generate correctly formatted Canvas files, Dataview queries, and Meta Bind widgets.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol) Support:** Integrated guides for setting up Obsidian MCP servers, allowing external agents to safely read and write to your vault.
- **Local LLM Integration:** Full support for **Ollama**, enabling you to "chat with your vault" privately and offline using Smart Connections.

---

### 📊 The "Bento Box" Dashboard

Information visibility is critical. OmniVault AI features a **Control Center** built with a "Brutalist Minimalist" design language.

Powered by **DataviewJS** and **Meta Bind**, the dashboard provides a high-density "Bento Box" layout that visualizes:
1.  **Active Workflows:** Real-time project status and modified dates.
2.  **Quick Actions:** One-click buttons to spawn new daily logs or run custom automation scripts.
3.  **Global Knowledge Base:** A live view of recently synthesized notes and literature.

---

### 📈 Metric-Driven Daily Logs

Your daily note should be more than a todo list. In OmniVault AI, the daily log is a **performance tracker**. Using interactive progress bars and toggles, I track:

- **Ultradian Cycles:** Monitoring focus windows to prevent burnout.
- **Study Ratings:** Quantifying learning sessions.
- **Habit Streaks:** Visualizing consistency in meditation, exercise, and coding.

All these metrics automatically roll up into **Weekly and Monthly Reviews**, providing an empirical look at my growth and productivity trends.
